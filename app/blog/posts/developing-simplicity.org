** Fractions traumatize way too many people

Forgive me, for saying that math is one of the most natural things in the world --- as natural as storytelling and imagination and walking talking primates. But in an 8th grade classroom, 75% of the students won't agree [fn:1]. And that was before covid.

These eighth graders then grow up to become adults. And 1 of 3 american adults don't understand fractions. They never got around to it. Why? The simple answer is that our way of representing fractions is broken. The most successful remedies include [fn:2]:
